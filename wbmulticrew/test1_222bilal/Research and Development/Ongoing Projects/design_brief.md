# Design Brief for Ongoing Projects Section of the BKF Pharma Website

## Overview
The Ongoing Projects section will provide visitors with detailed information about the current research projects being undertaken by BKF Pharma. This section aims to highlight the innovative work being done in cancer treatment, without revealing sensitive details. It will include visually appealing elements to engage the target audience, which includes potential partners, investors, medical professionals, researchers, and the general public.

## Layout
The Ongoing Projects section will be structured as follows:
1. **Section Header**
   - Title: "Ongoing Projects"
   - Subtitle: A brief description highlighting the innovative nature of the projects.
2. **Project Cards**
   - Each project will be displayed in a card format, including an image, project title, brief description, and a "Learn More" button.
3. **Call-to-Action**
   - A section at the bottom encouraging visitors to get in touch for more detailed information or partnership opportunities.

## Color Scheme
- **Primary Colors:**
  - Deep Blue (#003366): For section headers and primary buttons.
  - White (#FFFFFF): For background and text.
- **Secondary Colors:**
  - Light Gray (#F2F2F2): For card backgrounds and subtle accents.
  - Teal (#008080): For secondary buttons and focus effects.
- **Accent Colors:**
  - Bright Green (#00FF00): For call-to-action buttons and highlights.

## Typography
- **Primary Font:** Helvetica Neue, sans-serif
  - **Headings:** Bold, uppercase
  - **Body Text:** Regular weight
  - **Captions and Smaller Text:** Light weight
- **Font Sizes:** Responsive typography to ensure readability on all devices. Adjust font sizes based on device screen size.

## Iconography
- Use simple, universally recognizable icons to enhance navigation and user experience.

## User Interface Components
### Buttons
- **Primary Button:** 
  - Background Color: Bright Green (#00FF00)
  - Text Color: White (#FFFFFF)
  - Border Radius: 5px
  - Padding: 10px 20px
  - Hover Effect: Slight darkening of the button color
- **Secondary Button:**
  - Background Color: Teal (#008080)
  - Text Color: White (#FFFFFF)
  - Border Radius: 5px
  - Padding: 10px 20px
  - Hover Effect: Slight darkening of the button color

### Cards
- **Background Color:** White (#FFFFFF)
- **Border:** 1px solid Light Gray (#F2F2F2)
- **Border Radius:** 10px
- **Shadow:** Subtle box-shadow for depth
- **Padding:** 20px
- **Hover Effect:** Slight lift and shadow enhancement to indicate interactivity

## Interactions and Animations
- **Smooth Scrolling:** Implement smooth scrolling for a better user experience.
- **Hover Effects:** Subtle hover effects on buttons, links, and cards to indicate interactivity.
- **Load Animations:** Light fade-in animations for content as it loads to enhance visual appeal without causing distraction.

## Image Requirements
1. **Project Images**
   - Description: High-quality images that represent the essence of each project. These could be abstract visuals, futuristic graphics, or images of researchers at work.
   - Style: Abstract, futuristic visuals with a human element (e.g., scientists in lab coats, researchers using advanced equipment).
   - Quantity: One image per project card.

## Content Requirements
1. **Section Header**
   - Title: "Ongoing Projects"
   - Subtitle: "Explore our innovative research projects aimed at revolutionizing cancer treatment."

   - **Project Title:** A concise, engaging title for each project.

   - **Learn More Button:** Text: "Learn More"

3. **Call-to-Action**
   - Text: "Interested in learning more or partnering with us? Get in touch today!"
   - Button Text: "Contact Us"

By following this design brief, the development team can create a visually appealing, informative, and engaging Ongoing Projects section that effectively communicates BKF Pharma's innovative work and attracts potential partners and investors.
