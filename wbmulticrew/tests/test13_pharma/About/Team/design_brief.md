# Team Section Design Brief for BKF Pharma Website

## Introduction
This design brief provides detailed guidelines for the creation of the Team section on the BKF Pharma website. The Team section will highlight key team members and their roles within the company. The design will be modern, sleek, and professional, aligning with the overall aesthetic of the website. This section will also emphasize the human element, showcasing the individuals behind the innovative work in cancer treatment.

## Design Elements

### Color Scheme
- **Primary Colors:**
  - **Deep Blue (#003366):** Conveys trust, professionalism, and reliability.
  - **Bright Cyan (#00BFFF):** Represents innovation, technology, and a forward-thinking approach.
- **Secondary Colors:**
  - **Light Grey (#F0F0F0):** Provides a clean and minimalist background.
  - **White (#FFFFFF):** Enhances readability and provides a fresh, modern feel.
  - **Soft Green (#66CDAA):** Adds a touch of warmth and humanity, symbolizing health and growth.
- **Accent Colors:**
  - **Vibrant Orange (#FFA500):** Used sparingly for call-to-action buttons and key highlights to draw attention.

### Typography
- **Primary Font:**
  - **Open Sans:** A modern, sans-serif font that is clean and highly readable.
- **Secondary Font:**
  - **Roboto Slab:** A serif font used for headings and subheadings to add a touch of elegance and professionalism.
- **Font Sizes:**
  - **Headings:** Use a hierarchical scale (e.g., H1, H2, H3) to maintain consistency and readability. Exact sizes will be responsive based on the user's device and screen size.
  - **Body Text:** Maintain a size that ensures readability across all devices.

### Layout
- **Section Layout:**
  - **Header:** A section title "Our Team" with a brief introductory text about the importance of the team in BKF Pharma's mission.
  - **Grid Layout:** Use a responsive grid layout to display team members. Each team member will be showcased in a card format.
  - **Cards:** Each card will include a profile image, name, position, and a brief bio of the team member. Cards will be interactive, with hover effects to provide additional information.

### Imagery
- **Profile Images:**
  - **Description:** Professional, high-resolution images of team members.
  - **Prompt for AI Model:** "Generate a high-resolution professional headshot of a scientist/researcher in a lab coat, with a clean, neutral background."
- **Background Images:**
  - **Description:** Subtle abstract visuals or a light gradient background that does not compete with the profile images.
  - **Prompt for AI Model:** "Generate a subtle abstract background with a futuristic feel, using light grey and soft green tones."

### Micro Interactions and Hover Effects
- **Hover Effects on Cards:**
  - **Image Zoom:** Slight zoom-in effect on the profile image.
  - **Card Elevation:** Increase in shadow and slight elevation to indicate interactivity.
  - **Text Highlight:** Change in text color to Bright Cyan for the name and position.
- **Interactive Elements:**
  - **Read More Links:** Links to detailed profiles or LinkedIn pages of the team members. These links will underline and change color on hover.

### User Interface Components
- **Buttons:**
  - **Primary Button:** Deep Blue background with white text for main actions.
  - **Secondary Button:** Light Grey background with Deep Blue text for secondary actions.
  - **Hover Effects:** Subtle color change to Bright Cyan to indicate interactivity.
- **Forms:**
  - **Contact Form:** If included in the Team section, it should follow the same design principles as the main contact form.
  - **Input Fields:** Light Grey background with a Deep Blue border. Focus state changes border to Bright Cyan.
- **Navigation:**
  - Ensure that the Team section can be easily accessed from the main navigation menu.

## Content Requirements
- **Section Header:**
  - **Title:** "Our Team"
  - **Intro Text:** A brief introduction about the teamâ€™s role and contribution to the company's mission. (Approx. 50-75 words)
- **Team Member Cards:**
  - **Name:** Full name of the team member.
  - **Position:** Their role within the company.
  - **Bio:** A brief biography highlighting their background, expertise, and contributions. (Approx. 100-150 words per team member)
  - **Read More Link:** Optional link to a detailed profile or LinkedIn page.

## Conclusion
The Team section of the BKF Pharma website will be designed to highlight the key team members and their contributions in a professional and engaging manner. By combining modern design elements with a human touch, this section will help build trust and credibility with potential partners, investors, and the general public.

This design brief provides a comprehensive guide for the design team to create a cohesive and visually appealing Team section that meets the user's requirements and expectations.
