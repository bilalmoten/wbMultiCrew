# Contact Form Section Design Brief for BKF Pharma Website

## 1. Overview
This design brief outlines the detailed design elements and guidelines for the Contact Form section of the BKF Pharma website. The primary goal of this section is to facilitate communication between BKF Pharma and potential partners and investors. This brief serves as a comprehensive guide for developers, content writers, and image artists to create a cohesive and visually appealing Contact Form section.

## 2. Design and Branding Guidelines

### 2.1 Design Style
- **Overall Style:** Modern, sleek design to reflect innovation and cutting-edge technology.
- **Imagery:** 
  - **Primary:** Abstract, futuristic visuals to evoke a sense of innovation.
  - **Secondary:** Images of people, such as scientists, researchers, or patients, to add a human touch and convey empathy.
- **Tone:** Inspirational and motivational, highlighting the potential impact of BKF Pharma's work on cancer treatment.

### 2.2 Color Scheme
- **Primary Colors:** 
  - **Shades of Blue:** Symbolizing trust, professionalism, and innovation.
    - Examples: #1E90FF (Dodger Blue), #4682B4 (Steel Blue)
  - **Accents of Green:** Symbolizing health and growth.
    - Examples: #32CD32 (Lime Green), #228B22 (Forest Green)
- **Secondary Colors:** 
  - **Neutral Colors:** White and grey for a clean, minimalist look.
    - Examples: #FFFFFF (White), #D3D3D3 (Light Grey), #A9A9A9 (Dark Grey)

### 2.3 Typography
- **Primary Font:** Modern sans-serif font for headings to convey simplicity and modernity.
  - Examples: Helvetica, Arial, Open Sans
- **Secondary Font:** Clean serif font for body text to ensure readability and a professional appearance.
  - Examples: Georgia, Times New Roman, Merriweather

### 2.4 User Interface Components
- **Form Fields:**
  - Style: Rounded corners, light grey borders, ample padding for ease of use.
  - Input Fields: Light grey background with a dark grey border, clear labels placed above each field.
  - Text Area: Similar styling to input fields but larger to accommodate longer messages.
- **Buttons:** 
  - Style: Rounded corners with slight shadows for a modern look.
  - Primary Button Color: Blue with white text.
  - Secondary Button Color: Green with white text.
  - Hover Effect: Slight darkening of the button color to indicate interactivity.
- **Labels:** 
  - Placement: Above the input fields.
  - Style: Clear, concise, and using the primary sans-serif font.

### 2.5 Basic Design Guidelines
- **Consistency:** Ensure a consistent look and feel by adhering to the defined color schemes, typography, and UI components.
- **Whitespace:** Use ample whitespace to create a clean and uncluttered layout, enhancing readability and user experience.
- **Responsiveness:** Design with a mobile-first approach, ensuring the section is fully responsive and functions seamlessly on all devices, including desktops, tablets, and mobile phones.

### 2.6 Interactions and Animations
- **Hover Effects:** Subtle and smooth transitions for buttons and links to indicate interactivity.
- **Focus States:** Clear focus states for form fields to improve accessibility and user experience.
- **Submission Feedback:** Provide feedback upon form submission, such as a success message or error indication.

## 3. Content Requirements
- **Headline:** "Get in Touch with Us"
  - Tone: Inspirational and inviting.
- **Subheadline:** "We are here to answer any questions you may have about our research and partnerships."
  - Tone: Professional and reassuring.
- **Form Fields:**
  - Name (Input Field)
  - Email (Input Field)
  - Subject (Input Field)
  - Message (Text Area)
- **Submit Button Text:** "Send Message"
  - Tone: Clear and action-oriented.
- **Contact Information:** 
  - Email: info@bkfpharma.com
  - Phone: +1 (800) 123-4567
  - Address: 123 Pharma St., BioCity, USA

## 4. Image Requirements
- **Background Image for Contact Form Section:** 
  - Description: Abstract, futuristic visual with a blue and green color palette to evoke innovation and trust.
  - Usage: To serve as a backdrop for the Contact Form section, enhancing the modern and sleek design.
- **Human Element Image:**
  - Description: Image of a scientist working in a lab, conveying a sense of innovation and human touch.
  - Usage: To be placed alongside the contact form to add a human touch and emphasize the scientific focus of BKF Pharma.

## 5. Layout
- **Section Layout:** 
  - Two-column layout with the form on the left and the image on the right.
  - Headline and subheadline centered above the form.
  - Contact information placed below the form.
- **Form Layout:** 
  - Vertical alignment of form fields with clear labels.
  - Submit button placed at the bottom center of the form.
- **Image Placement:** 
  - Image aligned with the top of the form to create a balanced visual hierarchy.

---

This design brief provides a comprehensive overview of the design elements and guidelines for the Contact Form section of the BKF Pharma website. By following these guidelines, designers, developers, content writers, and image artists can create a cohesive and visually appealing section that effectively facilitates communication with potential partners and investors.
