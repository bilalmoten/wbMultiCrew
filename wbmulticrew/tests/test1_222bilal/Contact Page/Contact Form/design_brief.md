# Contact Form Section Design Brief for BKF Pharma Website

## Overview
The Contact Form section of the BKF Pharma website is intended to facilitate communication between potential partners, investors, medical professionals, researchers, and the company. This section should be user-friendly, easily accessible, and visually appealing to encourage visitors to reach out.

## Section Layout
The Contact Form section will be structured as follows:
1. **Header**: A brief introduction text encouraging visitors to get in touch.
2. **Contact Form**: A form with fields for name, email, subject, and message.
3. **Contact Information**: BKF Pharma’s email addresses, phone numbers, and physical address.
4. **Map**: An embedded map showing the location of BKF Pharma's office (optional).

## Content Requirements
- **Header Text**: "Get in Touch with Us"
  - Subtext: "We are here to answer any questions you may have about our research and innovations. Reach out to us, and we’ll respond as soon as we can."
- **Form Fields**:
  - **Name**: Placeholder text "Your Name"
  - **Email**: Placeholder text "Your Email"
  - **Subject**: Placeholder text "Subject"
  - **Message**: Placeholder text "Your Message"
  - **Submit Button**: Text "Send Message"
- **Contact Information**:
  - **Email**: "contact@bkfpharma.com"
  - **Phone**: "+1 (555) 123-4567"
  - **Address**: "123 Pharma Blvd, Innovation City, CA 94043"
- **Map**: Optional embedded Google Map showing the company’s location.

## Image Requirements
- **Background Image**: A subtle, abstract, and futuristic visual that aligns with the branding guidelines. Description for AI image generation:
  - "A sleek, abstract background with futuristic elements. Soft blue and teal hues to evoke innovation and high-tech capabilities. Include subtle geometric shapes and gradients to add depth."
- **Form Icon**: An icon representing contact or communication. Description for AI image generation:
  - "A simple, modern icon of an envelope or chat bubble, in teal color, to represent communication and contact."

## Design Elements

### Color Scheme
- **Primary Colors**:
  - Deep Blue (#003366): For headers and form titles.
  - White (#FFFFFF): For form backgrounds and text fields.
- **Secondary Colors**:
  - Light Gray (#F2F2F2): For borders and subtle accents.
  - Teal (#008080): For input field focus borders and icons.
- **Accent Colors**:
  - Bright Green (#00FF00): For the submit button.

### Typography
- **Primary Font**: Helvetica Neue, sans-serif
  - **Headings**: Bold, uppercase for the header text.
  - **Body Text**: Regular weight for form labels and instructions.
  - **Input Fields**: Light weight for placeholder text.
- **Font Sizes**:
  - **Header Text**: 24px
  - **Subtext**: 16px
  - **Form Labels and Input Text**: 14px
  - **Button Text**: 14px

### User Interface Components
- **Form Fields**:
  - Border: 1px solid Light Gray (#F2F2F2)
  - Border Radius: 5px
  - Padding: 10px
  - Focus Effect: Border color changes to Teal (#008080)
- **Submit Button**:
  - Background Color: Bright Green (#00FF00)
  - Text Color: White (#FFFFFF)
  - Border Radius: 5px
  - Padding: 10px 20px
  - Hover Effect: Slight darkening of the button color

### Contact Information Section
- Background Color: Light Gray (#F2F2F2)
- Padding: 20px
- Border Radius: 10px
- Text Color: Deep Blue (#003366)

### Map (Optional)
- Embed a responsive Google Map iframe with a border radius of 10px and a subtle box-shadow for depth.

## Interactions and Animations
- **Form Fields**:
  - Smooth focus transition with color change to Teal (#008080).
- **Submit Button**:
  - Slight darkening on hover to indicate interactivity.
  - Click animation: Button slightly depresses to provide feedback that the form is being submitted.
- **Contact Information**:
  - Subtle fade-in animation when the section loads to draw attention without distraction.

## General Design Guidelines
- **Consistency**: Maintain a consistent look and feel across the Contact Form section and the rest of the website.
- **Whitespace**: Use ample whitespace to avoid clutter and enhance readability.
- **Responsiveness**: Ensure the section is fully responsive, adapting seamlessly to various screen sizes.
- **Accessibility**: Follow best practices to ensure the form is accessible to all users, including those with disabilities.

By adhering to these guidelines, the Contact Form section will be a functional, visually appealing, and user-friendly component of the BKF Pharma website, encouraging visitors to make contact and fostering communication with potential partners and investors.
