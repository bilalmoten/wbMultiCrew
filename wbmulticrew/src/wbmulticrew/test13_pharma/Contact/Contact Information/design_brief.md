# BKF Pharma Website - Contact Information Section Design Brief

## Introduction
This design brief outlines the detailed requirements for the Contact Information section of the BKF Pharma website. The section will provide visitors with multiple ways to contact the company, ensuring a professional and welcoming approach. The design will align with the overall modern, sleek, and inspirational tone of the website.

## Design Elements

### Layout
- **Section Structure:**
  - **Header:** A clear and concise title, such as "Get in Touch" or "Contact Us".
  - **Contact Information:** Display email, phone number, and physical address.
  - **Contact Form:** A user-friendly form for visitors to send messages directly through the website.
  - **Map:** An embedded map showing the company's physical location.
- **Grid System:** Use a responsive grid system to ensure the section looks great on all devices. Typically a 12-column grid layout.
- **Spacing:** Adequate padding and margins to ensure the section is not cluttered and is easily readable.

### Color Scheme
- **Primary Colors:**
  - **Deep Blue (#003366):** Used for text headers and primary buttons.
  - **Bright Cyan (#00BFFF):** Used for accenting and hover effects.
- **Secondary Colors:**
  - **Light Grey (#F0F0F0):** Background of the contact form and map area.
  - **White (#FFFFFF):** Main background color for the section and form fields.
  - **Soft Green (#66CDAA):** Highlights and subtle accents in the contact details.
- **Accent Colors:**
  - **Vibrant Orange (#FFA500):** Call-to-action buttons and key interactive elements.

### Typography
- **Primary Font:**
  - **Open Sans:** Used for all body text and form labels.
- **Secondary Font:**
  - **Roboto Slab:** Used for section headers and subheaders.
- **Font Sizes:**
  - **Header (H2):** 36px, Bold.
  - **Subheader (H3):** 28px, Semi-Bold.
  - **Body Text:** 16px, Regular.
  - **Form Labels:** 14px, Medium.

### Iconography
- **Icons:**
  - **Email Icon:** Simple envelope icon, consistent with the site's modern style.
  - **Phone Icon:** Classic phone handset icon.
  - **Location Icon:** Pin icon for address.
  - **Map Marker Icon:** Custom map marker in the company’s primary color.
- **Style:** Line icons with a modern, minimalist design.

### User Interface Components
- **Contact Form:**
  - **Fields:** Name, Email, Subject, Message.
  - **Button:** "Send Message" with primary button styling (Deep Blue background, white text).
  - **Validation:** Real-time validation with error messages in Soft Green.
  - **Focus State:** Bright Cyan border for focused input fields.
- **Map:**
  - **Embed:** Google Maps or similar service showing the company's location.
  - **Marker:** Custom marker in Deep Blue to match the site’s color scheme.
- **Hover Effects:**
  - **Buttons:** Slight color change to Bright Cyan on hover.
  - **Links:** Underline effect on hover.
  - **Icons:** Slight scale-up effect on hover.

### Imagery
- **Background Image:**
  - **Description:** An abstract, futuristic image that evokes innovation and cutting-edge technology.
  - **Usage:** Full-width background behind the contact form and map.
- **Profile Images:**
  - **Description:** Professional, high-resolution images of team members who might be points of contact.
  - **Usage:** Small circular images next to contact details.

### Animations and Interactions
- **Animations:**
  - **Fade-In:** Smooth fade-in effect for the section as it enters the viewport.
  - **Slide-In:** Contact form fields slide in from the left.
  - **Bounce:** Map marker bounces slightly upon page load.
- **Interactions:**
  - **Form Submission:** Clear feedback upon form submission (e.g., "Thank you for your message. We will get back to you soon.").
  - **Map Interactivity:** Allow users to zoom in/out and move the map.
